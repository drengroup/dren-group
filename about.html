The story
2023

Built the platform

Engineered the TADR platform from scratch — a multi-tenant SaaS purpose-built for travel and tourism operators. The foundation for everything that followed.

2024

First client on the platform

Deployed TADR for a travel agency client in the Dominican Republic. Booking flows, DGII compliance, customer management — all running on the infrastructure we built.

2025

Custom builds & DGII ecosystem

Built CEBA Scuba as a fully custom system — owned outright by the client. Developed the DGII compliance engine and a suite of independent, domain-specific modules. Proved custom-build and platform models both work.

2026

BlackBeards & growing the ecosystem

Launched BlackBeards Tours on TADR. Formalizing Dren Group as the parent entity. Opening infrastructure engineering to other specialized businesses across industries and markets.
