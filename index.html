Scuba Tourism · Custom Build

CEBA
Scuba

Scuba tourism. Engineered for complexity.

The challenge

Existing software comes with expensive subscriptions and is nearly impossible to modify. With CEBA, we engineered the entire system from scratch — and the client owns it outright. No recurring license, no vendor limits.

What we built

A custom operations dashboard handling the full complexity: booking management, certification tracking, equipment inventory, digital waiver signing, customer profiles, and calendar coordination across multiple dive sites.

"This is what we engineered for a specialized vertical. Every domain has this level of complexity. We know how to build for it."
Travel & Tours · TADR Platform

BlackBeards
Tours

Travel operations. Automated and profitable.

The challenge

Tour operations need seamless booking flows, real-time availability, customer communication, DGII compliance, and revenue optimization — working together, not stitched apart.

Zona Sur
